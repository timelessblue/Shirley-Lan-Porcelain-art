                                    5Spontaneous Poetry Facebook fan pagesTimeless BlueOffical webShirley Lan majored in Fashion Design and Business Management, with over 30 years of professional experience in the European international fashion industry. A serendipitous encounter led her into the world of porcelain painting, where she later founded Timeless Blue Artwork Co. and served as the convener of the Contemporary Originality Porcelain Artist Union. Her creations are marked by boundless imagination and masterful technique. Elegantly timeless, her works blend the poetic grace of Eastern classical culture with the romantic aesthetics of the West. With diverse, nonrepetitive forms, her art evokes deep emotional and intellectual resonance. Her original %u201ccloud dyeing%u201d and %u201csoil pile texture%u201d techniques create unique three-dimensional gradient s on porcelain sur faces, introducing an extraordinary breakthrough in contemporary porcelain painting.A passionate lover of poetry and literature, Shirley is committed to promoting dialogue between humanities and the arts. She manages the famous Facebook pages Spontaneous and Timeless Blue, and actively engages in art-related self-media video production. In collaboration with renowned kiln studios and art centers, she launched the art program Art Window, featuring video interviews with prominent artists and professionals to disseminate modern aesthetics.Noticing the public's lack of access to information about porcelain art, and moved to comfort people%u2019s hearts during the pandemic, Shirley initiated and delivered over 40 public lectures at Rotary Clubs and major cultural organizations, advocating for the beauty of porcelain painting through action.Since 2021, her works have been regularly exhibited at Veryartist Gallery and Tai-Hwa Pottery, Taiwan%u2019s largest porcelain gallery chain. In the same year, she launched the Timeless Blue brand and its official website. Starting in 2024, she was invited to hold monthly rotating exhibitions at SOGO Department Stores and, in November, held a month-long solo exhibition at Far Eastern Department Store's Grand Porcelain Gallery on the third floor, with an ongoing display and sale presence in the fourth-floor art space.On April 7, 2022, she became the first artist in Taiwan to issue porcelain NFTs, which sold out within six hours.Later that year, on December 20, she launched Taiwan%u2019s first comprehensive online course on porcelain art concepts and techniques.In September 2022, her work passed the stringent evaluation of the Fo Guang Yuan Museum headquarters, which operates 28 museums worldwide, and is scheduled to be exhibited at the Fo Guang Yuan Museum in Taipei in 2025.In 2023, she was invited to the Ode to the Master exhibition at Fo Guang Yuan Museum of Art in Taipei, where she created a commemorative porcelain piece titled The Great Vow of Compassion in honor of Master Hsing Yun. Weighing over 10 kilograms, the artwork is now part of the museum's permanent collection and was featured in media reports by Human TV and Fubao.In September 2023, she received the Cultural and Creative Elite Award from the Taipei Painting and Calligraphy Artists Professional Union.In June 2024, her work stood out among hundreds of entries and won the Gold Award at the 7th Japan International Art Exhibition.She is currently a member of the Asia Art Alliance, and is actively involved in organizing the 15th Asia Art Alliance Exhibition 2025 in Taoyuan, where she serves as Chair of the Media Promotion Program.
